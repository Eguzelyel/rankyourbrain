Understanding IQ Scores

IQ (Intelligence Quotient) is a measure of a person's reasoning ability. It is meant to gauge how well someone can use information and logic to answer questions or make predictions.

In Rank Your Brain, we calculate two types of IQ scores:

  • STEM IQ: Based on your performance in mathematics, physics, biology, chemistry.
  • Verbal IQ: Based on your performance in language, geography, history, and psychology.

How We Calculate Your IQ

In Rank Your Brain, your IQ scores are calculated based on your performance in various subject areas:

  1. We track your correct and incorrect answers in each subject.
  2. Each question has a specific weight for STEM and Verbal categories.
  3. Correct answers add points to your score, while incorrect answers may slightly decrease it.
  4. The final score is normalized to fit the standard IQ scale with a mean of 100 and a standard deviation of 15.

Keep in mind that these scores are meant to be a fun way to track your progress and are not equivalent to professional IQ assessments.
